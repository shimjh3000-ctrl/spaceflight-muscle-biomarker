# PRISMA 2020 Search Strategy

## Databases queried (last update: April 2026)

- PubMed (NLM) — https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/
- EMBASE (Elsevier) — institutional access
- Web of Science Core Collection (Clarivate) — institutional access

## Search terms (Boolean)

```
( "spaceflight" OR "microgravity" OR "weightlessness" OR "hindlimb unloading"
  OR "hindlimb suspension" OR "bed rest" )
AND
( "skeletal muscle" OR "myocyte" OR "myotube" OR "myofibre" OR "myofiber"
  OR "atrophy" OR "sarcopenia" OR "disuse" )
AND
( "transcriptom*" OR "RNA-seq" OR "microarray" OR "differentially expressed"
  OR "gene expression" OR "DEG" )
```

## Screening levels

1. Title + abstract (deduplicated; rayyan.qcri.org)
2. Full-text eligibility check
3. Data extraction (FDR, Log2FC, sample size, model, tissue)
4. Quality assessment (modified Newcastle-Ottawa for transcriptomics)
